Diabetes Prediction System



Diabetes is considered as one of the deadliest and chronic diseases which causes an increase in blood sugar. Many complications occur if diabetes remains untreated and unidentified.Early prediction of diabetes is quite challenging task for medical practitioners.

The rise in machine learning approaches solves this critical problem. The motive of this system is to design a model which can predict the likelihood of diabetes in patients with maximum accuracy.

The System requires different input parameters to predict whether the person suffers from Diabetes or not.These features are:
1. Number of times pregnant
2. Plasma glucose concentration
3. Diastolic blood pressure
4. Skin fold thickness
5. 2-Hour serum insulin
6. BMI
7. Diabetes pedigree function
8. Age in years
